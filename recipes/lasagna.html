Lasagna Recipe

Description

This might be the world's best lasagna!

Ingerdients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato cans
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
